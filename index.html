The Algorithm

What ITA ALGO Does & Why

Gold Market Specialist

We trade XAUUSD — that’s the price of gold measured in US dollars. Gold is one of the most actively traded markets in the world, giving our algorithm plenty of short-term opportunities to work with.

Strict Rules, No Guessing

Every trade follows a clear set of rules. The algorithm always sets a “Stop Loss” (an automatic safety net that limits how much you can lose on any single trade) and a “Take Profit” (an automatic sell point that locks in your gains).

Built-In Safety Nets

The algorithm decides how much to risk on each trade based on your account size — like a poker player who never bets more than they can afford to lose. There’s also an account-wide safety switch that pauses everything if your balance drops too far.

Automated Discipline

No second-guessing, no panic selling, no “I’ll skip this one.” The system runs around the clock, 5 days a week, executing trades instantly without fatigue.
