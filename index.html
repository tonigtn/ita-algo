01 — The Algorithm

What Is ITA ALGO?

Three core technologies working together. Here is what each one means — explained simply.

01

Genetic Algorithm

Technical: The strategy parameters were optimised through an evolutionary process — thousands of parameter combinations were tested, the weakest eliminated, and the strongest “survived” to form the final system.

Real-Life Analogy
Imagine running 10,000 different chess strategies in simulation. You observe which ones win, discard the losers, combine the winners, and repeat — until only the strongest strategy remains. ITA ALGO wasn’t designed by hand. It was evolved to win.
✓ Result: A strategy that has survived extreme market conditions that would destroy manually-designed systems.
02

Institutional Order Flow Positioning

Technical: The algorithm identifies and aligns with large institutional orders — the “smart money” moves made by banks and hedge funds — using price action patterns that signal where these large players are accumulating or distributing positions.

Real-Life Analogy
Imagine you’re on a highway and you can see exactly which lane the heavy trucks are about to move into, before they signal. Banks and hedge funds move markets — ITA ALGO reads their footprints and rides alongside them.
✓ Result: Trades placed in the direction of market momentum — significantly improving win quality.
03

Retracement Synchronisation

Technical: Instead of chasing breakouts, the algorithm waits for price to pull back to a key level within the broader trend — entering at the optimal moment when risk is lowest and potential reward is highest.

Real-Life Analogy
Think of a surfer waiting for the perfect wave. A beginner jumps on every wave and wipes out. An expert reads the ocean, waits patiently, and only paddles when conditions are ideal. ITA ALGO catches the wave at its base, not at its peak.
✓ Result: Entry points with tighter stop-losses and better reward-to-risk ratios — the key driver behind the 20.57 Sharpe Ratio.
